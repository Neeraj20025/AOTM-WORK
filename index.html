About Digital India 2048

Our mission is to create a digitally empowered society where technology is accessible, safe, and sustainable. We partner with local governments, private sector innovators, and civil society to deliver high-impact programs.

Green tech & sustainability

Everything we build follows energy-efficient and low-carbon principles — from solar-powered kiosks to green data centers.

Inclusive design

Services in multiple regional languages, offline-first apps, and accessibility features to ensure nobody is left behind.

Key Services

Telemedicine

Remote consultations and electronic health records for rural clinics.

eLearning

Localized courseware and community learning centers.

Digital Payments

Secure, low-cost transaction infrastructure for MSMEs and citizens.

Impact so far

From digital public infrastructure to last-mile services — we focus on measurable outcomes.

  • Increased digital literacy in 7,500 villages.
  • Reduced time-to-service for welfare schemes by 40% in pilot districts.
  • Created 150,000 local micro-entrepreneur opportunities.
